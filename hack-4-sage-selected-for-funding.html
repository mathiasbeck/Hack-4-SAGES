Hack-4-SAGES selected for funding

We are excited to share the great information that we received yesterday, Tuesday 01 July 2025.

The Hack-4-SAGES project was selected for funding via the ENHANCE Testbed seed fund. The application was rated with 97.6 / 100 possible points and is ranked #1 out of 10 funded projects. We are very grateful to the ENHANCE Alliance for the generous support.

If you are interested to know about the other projects that were selected for funding, you may consult the ENHANCE Testbed website : https://www.wut.cwm.pw.edu.pl/testbed/
